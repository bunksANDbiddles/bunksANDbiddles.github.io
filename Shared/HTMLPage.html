At BBS, we know that students succeed in life when they are fully prepared                                        Victoria Adembesa is a member of the Bunks & Biddles School since 2012. She earned her bachelor’s degree from Moi university.   Victoria became Head of School in 2013 after 3 years of teaching and leadership positions at different institutions.   Victoria has trained and mentored various young people with The Navigators International Ministries, she has taught Math and Biology courses over the years, has been involved and led several clubs and club programs and simply loves working with young people. In her words, “The Chinese proverb nails it – if you do something you love, you never work a day in your life! I love spending my days the way that I do!”
